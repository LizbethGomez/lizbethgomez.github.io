Who am I?

NYC 2022

I’m currently a doctoral candidate at Drexel University’s Dornsife School of Public Health in Philadelphia and a GATHER Fellow. I am working under the advisement of Dr. Jane Clougherty on determining pharmaceutical treatment efficacy modification by Social and Environmental exposures. Previously, I received an MPH in Environmental Health Science from Mailman School of Public Health and a Bachelors of Science from the City College of New York. ~~~~~

My research interests are in epidemiological and biostatistical methods as well as asthma disparities. I first developed these interests as a fellow in the Puerto Rico IPE Service Learning Fellowship, where I got to work inter-professionally with a multidisciplinary team and develop a community-based participatory and service learning research project in San Juan, Puerto Rico. These interests have continued beyond my experience as a fellow, and have become a motivation for producing meaningful and reproducible research.

I am a huge foodie, I visit NYC (where I am from) as often as my PhD training allows. When I am not gardening, you can find me cuddling with my Bichon-Frise puppy, Lilu, baking, reading (fiction, too) or coding in R.
